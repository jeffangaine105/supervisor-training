Why Conflict Management Matters

Most conflicts don't start as conflicts. They start as misunderstandings, pressure, or unmet expectations. Left unmanaged, they lead to QC drops, rework, low morale, and client concerns.

Supervisors who manage conflict well:
✓ Prevent small issues from escalating
✓ Protect work quality and standards
✓ Keep high performers engaged and respected
✓ Maintain client and team confidence
